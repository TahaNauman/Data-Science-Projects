# Netflix EDA

Exploratory Data Analysis of Netflix titles to uncover trends in Movies and TV Shows, including content types, genres, ratings, countries, actors, and durations.

**Dataset:** [`netflix_titles.csv`](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/shivamb/netflix-shows) from Kaggle.  

**Tools:** Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn.  

**Key Insights:**  
- The US produces the most Netflix content.  
- TV Shows are growing faster than Movies in recent years.  
- Drama and Comedy are the most common genres.  
- Most movies are 80–120 minutes; TV Shows usually have 1–3 seasons.  

Open `Netflix_EDA.ipynb` to explore the dataset and visualizations.
